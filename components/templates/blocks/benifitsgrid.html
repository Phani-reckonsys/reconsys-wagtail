Benefits of working in Reckonsys

We help bring out the best in employees by providing them with the Best Opportunities and challenges needed for growth.

Health

We provide Medical Insurance for you and your family. You take care of your work and we take care of you and your family’s health. We also provide Personal Accident Insurance for all our team members..

Wealth

We offer competitive salaries because we recognize the value you add is often priceless. We encourage our team members to attain financial freedom by paid access to our exclusive online mutual fund platform.

Happiness

We work hard, and at the same time, we want you to have a life outside of work. We are always looking for ways to make our workplace a happier one so that you don’t have to experience the Monday Morning Blues.

Knowledge

We offer an annual learning allowance to our team members for their professional development. We value your individual growth, as it is essential for our organization's growth.
